Chalfont Eagles Badminton Club

The Chalfont Eagles Badminton Club is a prestigous badminton club for 12-16 year olds. Being the biggest junior badminton club in Buckinghamshire, Chalfont Eagles has won many league titles in it's under 12's, under 14's and under 16's badminton squads.

This makes Chalfont Eagles the perfect badminton club for beginner players who want to try to learn the game, or more experienced players who want to use the clubs expert coaching in order to improve at the game. Wether you are new to the sport, or a more advanced player, Chalfont Eagles Badminton Club can help you develop your skills; on and off the court.
